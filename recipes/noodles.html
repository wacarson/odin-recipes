Spaghetti Chicken Recipe

Spaghetti Chicken

It starts off looking like this

Description

It is boiled noodles with some salt and a piece of chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Box of Spaghetti
  • 1/4lb Chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Water

Directions

  1. Fill pot with water and add salt
  2. Bring water to a boil
  3. Add spaghetti and boil for 6 minutes
  4. At the same time, grill a piece of chicken
  5. Drain cooked spaghetti and add piece of chicken
  6. Enjoy
