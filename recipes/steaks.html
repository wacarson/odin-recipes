Sloppy Steaks

Slop it up!

Description

A delicious steak. Just add water!

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Seasoning
  • Water
  • More Water

Directions

  1. Cook the steak with seasonings
  2. Serve the steak on a plate
  3. Dump water all over the steak
  4. Eat it as fast as you can
