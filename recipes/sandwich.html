Description

Bread with stuff

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Stuff
  • No Water

Directions

  1. Toast the bread if you want
  2. Prepare the stuff. Cut it up or get it out the jar, whatever is required
  3. Put the stuff on the bread slices (or toast)
  4. Eat it over a plate. Do not add water or get wet.
